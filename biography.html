About Me

Sarah is a senior at Northwestern University studying Cognitive Science, Legal Studies, and Human Computer Interaction. She is involved in a few pre-professional groups at Northwestern, including Women in Business, Women in Law, and Women in Computing. Sarah is also a member of the leadership team for both the Cognitive Science Club and the Waldron Student Alumni Connections Board. She was born and raised in Albuquerque, New Mexico with her three siblings. Sarah enjoys reading, digital art, creative writing, and playing tennis.
